[IT Resume Builder](https://resume-builder-ne.web.app/) is a premium web-based resume builder tailored specifically for IT professionals. It allows you to build, customize, and export clean, high-resolution resumes directly in your browser.

I was looking for a job and they asked to send a CV, so I was thinking about building a fresh, modern resume and decided to code this.

### Key Features
- **Modern Layout Templates:** Choose from different templates such as Modern Tech, Professional Sidebar Left, and Clean Minimalist.
- **Premium Typography & Styling:** Supports premium Google Fonts combinations (like Outfit/Inter, Space Grotesk/Inter, and JetBrains Mono) with customizable accent colors.
- **Perfect A4 Scaling:** Includes real-time page overflow warnings so you know exactly if your content fits within standard A4 print limits.
- **Flexible Exporters:** Download your data as JSON backup, export to Microsoft Word (.doc) format, or print directly to a clean PDF.
- **Data Privacy & Control:** All processing and storage are done entirely in your browser. No registration required, and you can clear or reload demo data at any time.
- **Bilingual Interface:** Toggle between English and Russian UI languages.
